Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

Afelu Guardian is built on the principle of anonymity and privacy. We collect minimal data and ensure complete separation between sponsors and end users.

Information We Collect

  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe. We do not store payment details.
  • Access Codes: Anonymous codes with no personal identifiers.
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymized service usage for system optimization.
  • Contact Forms: Name and email when you contact us voluntarily.

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers of end users
  • Location data or IP addresses of bot users
  • Chat content or messages
  • Any linkage between sponsors and end users

How We Use Information

  • Process payments and generate access codes
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve service reliability
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. All communications through our Telegram bot are encrypted by Telegram's protocols.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: Payment processing with their privacy policy
  • Telegram: Bot messaging with their privacy policy
  • Railway: Database hosting with their privacy policy

Your Rights

You can request deletion of any personal data we have collected. Contact us at support@afelu.com.
