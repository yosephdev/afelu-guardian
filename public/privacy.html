Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

Afelu Guardian is built on the principle of protecting student privacy and educational data. We collect minimal data necessary for providing quality AI education and maintain secure learning environments.

Information We Collect

  • Educational Records: Course progress, completion status, and certificates earned.
  • Learning Analytics: Anonymized learning patterns to improve course content.
  • Account Information: Optional email and name for course enrollment and certificates.
  • Contact Forms: Name and email when you contact us voluntarily.

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Sensitive personal information without consent
  • Biometric or identification data
  • Private learning content or notes
  • Financial information (all courses are free)

How We Use Information

  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Track course progress and issue certificates
  • Improve educational content and platform features
  • Provide student support and assistance

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect educational records. All student data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized educational staff.

Third-Party Services

  • Learning Management System: Course delivery platform
  • Certificate Generation: Digital credential services
  • Analytics: Educational performance tracking (anonymized)

Your Rights

You can access, update, or request deletion of your educational records. You also have the right to download your certificates and learning progress data. Contact us at support@afelu.com.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at support@afelu.com
