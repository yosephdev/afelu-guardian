Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience and understand how our service is used.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality, security, and payment processing. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our website to improve user experience. These are optional.

Third-Party Cookies

  • Stripe: Payment processing and fraud prevention
  • Google Fonts: Typography loading (no tracking)
  • Cloudflare: Security and performance optimization

Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (may affect website functionality)
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Delete cookies after each session
  • Manage cookies for specific websites

Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of essential cookies. Optional cookies require your explicit consent through our cookie banner.
